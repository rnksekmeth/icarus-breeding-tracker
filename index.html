Settings

🗑️ Clear All Data

Permanently deletes all animals, pairs, and goals. Your data file will be overwritten immediately. This cannot be undone.

⚠ This action is final. Export a CSV first if you want a backup.

📤 Export & Import

Export your herd as a CSV for spreadsheet viewing or external backup. Import a CSV to restore or bulk-add animals.

📸 Share Your Herd

Generate a shareable image of your animals — great for posting on forums or Discord. The image is copied to your clipboard automatically; a download button appears as a fallback.
